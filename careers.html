Title:Account Supervisor

Location:Los Angeles, CA

Arrowhead Advertising, a fast growing full-service automotive advertising agency based in Phoenix, is looking for a stand out, experienced Account Executive to help lead a group of new car dealer clients in the Los Angeles area.

Job Description:

Manage advertising and marketing plan development and execution for multiple automotive dealership businesses.

Responsibilities:
  • Provide sound monthly advertising recommendations based upon keen understanding of dealership business goals, sales objectives, market share position, inventory, competitive pressure and launches, automotive market conditions, and other local market, media and promotional opportunities
  • Plan, organize, implement and control the day-to-day processes of dealership accounts, including monthly media and creative plan presentations to client
  • Established trust-based relationships with clients generated from impeccable and responsible client service, regular communication and dealership visits
  • Provide immediate response to all client comments, inquiries and requests
  • Proactively initiate and mange timelines for all creative projects and media assignments
  • Develop knowledgeable understanding of national and regional advertising plans for all manufacturers
  • Manage dealer co-op program budget, billing, reconciliation and adherence to all advertising requirements and reimbursement processes
  • Ensure advertising and disclosures are consistent with state and local laws, regulations
  • Disseminate information in a timely and efficient manner to agency, clients and vendors
  • Display ongoing commitment and passion for client business growth through constant communication, responsiveness and expanding knowledge and expertise
  • Travel will generally involve driving trips to dealerships within a 1.5 hour driving radius. Overnight travel and overtime will be required.
Requirements:
  • Education: Bachelor degree in advertising, marketing, management, communications or equivalent, relevant work experience.
  • Work experience: 5+ Years experience at a full-service advertising agency with client interaction. Automotive category strongly preferred.
  • Skills: Must have experience with client interaction, strong presentation skills, firm grasp of financial management, understanding of media planning and buying possess, understanding digital and social media platforms, confidence to assertively and tactfully manage challenging clients, exceptional communication skills, agile, creative on both strategic and tactical level, organized and able to multi-task and keep up with up with active workload in fast paced retail environment.
  • The ability to work from a home office.
Compensation and Benefits:
  • Competitive base salary + commission
  • Excellent benefits package including 401K plan

Is this you? If so, email your resume to hr@arrowheadadv.com.

The best fit for us is someone who can create the big idea (or four) yet manages the 8th iteration of the last big idea. Someone to be mindful and yet be creative within the boundaries of the brand identities and standards we've already established for each of our clients. In other words, think inside the box and outside of it, too.

  • Ability to give creative and art direction based off of Creative Director needs.
  • Create new and copy edit scripts for broadcast television, radio, and digital mediums.

  • Participate in creative brainstorming. And be the storm.
  • Execute grammatically correct and engaging copy
  • Manage multiple projects and juggle own deliverables
  • Create exciting new concepts for new business pitches and prospects.
  • Desire to learn and grow and be part of our team.

  • 5+ years creative and copywriting experience, automotive a plus
